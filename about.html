BACKGROUND AND PHILOSOPHY

I'm a Software Developer & UX Designer based in Atlanta, Georgia.

After graduating in 2010 with a Bachelor’s Degree in Risk Management and Insurance at University of Georgia, I spent the last nine years working in the insurance industry as a claims consultant and underwriting analyst as well as a marketing account manager. Both positions required great analytical and marketing skills.

I always had a passion for creative design as well as data and problem solving. I spend the past year working as a freelance software developer acquiring various clients along the way. I acquired project and time management skills, as well as the ability to communicate with team members and clients while effectively meeting milestones and deadlines.

As a freelance web developer and designer I collaborated with several clients in various fields such as event production, meal prep business to hospitality settings. I have worked on a multitude of web and print based projects for a range of clients providing Web Design (Photoshop & Illustrator) and Development (PHP, HTML, CSS, LESS, JS, jQuery, React, API, Bootstrap, Responsive Layouts), eCommerce Solutions, Corporate Branding and Graphic Design.

I am currently working in freelance developer in Atlanta, Georgia. I am always available to discuss your project over the phone or via Skype. These days I enjoy spending my free time playing tennis, working out, cooking and playing with my pug, Gus.

I am a risk taker who has a passion for taking the hard road and aimlessly try to tackle a problem whether hard or impossible. As it goes, you learn from trying, even if you fail, not from accomplishing. I have an entrepreneurial spirit in combination with a mentoring mentality. I enjoy spreading my wealth of knowledge, but at the same time seek guidance when needed.
